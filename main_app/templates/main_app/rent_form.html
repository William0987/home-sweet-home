To add a suitable Google Maps Link:


1. Navigate to Google Maps and find the home location.


2. Under the overview tab, click on "Share".


3. Go to embed a map and you will find a HTML line to copy that will follow the given format: (iframe src="LINK" width="600" height="450" style="border:0;" allowfullscreen="" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer-when-downgrade).


4. Copy the "LINK" part of the line without the apostrophes or quotation marks.
